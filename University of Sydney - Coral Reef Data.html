University of Sydney - Coral Reef Data (2020-2025)

2020-2023: Fluctuating Recovery

Coral coverage recovered from its 2017 low, reaching 35.8% in 2023 (error range: 32.1%-39.5%).

2024: Catastrophic Bleaching

66% of coral bleached in early 2024, rising to 80% by April. By July, 53% of bleached corals died, with Acropora mortality at 95%.
